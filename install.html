Thank you for installing FantasyRig

FantasyRig allows you to view Sabermetrics for your players in ESPN and Yahoo fantasy baseball.

While looking at players via teams or free agency, click "FantasyRig 2017" to view this season's stats, "FantasyRig 2016" to view last season's stats, and "FantasyRig ROS" to view rest-of-season projected statistics.

Choose which stats to display and projection system to use on the settings page.

FAQ
What does stat x mean?

Refer to Fangraphs' glossary for hitting and pitching statistics

What projections are you using for ROS?

It defaults to Depth Charts, but there is an option to choose Steamers or ZiPS on the settings page.

How often does it update?

Daily

How do you project ROS fantasy points?

If you're in a points league, I collect your league settings and predict based on your selected projected system's ROS projections (default is Depth Charts). If you have scoring settings not projected by the system (e.g. GIDP, sacrifice flies, any fielding), then those stats will not factor into the projection.
